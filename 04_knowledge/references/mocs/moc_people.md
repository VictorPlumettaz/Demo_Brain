# MOC — People

A Map of Content is a page that holds nothing but links: an entrance to one corner of the
vault, written by hand, so that finding something does not depend on remembering a filename.

This one exists because people are what everything else docks onto. Meetings, tickets and
decisions all name someone, and from any of those names I want to be one click from "how do
I work with this person".
